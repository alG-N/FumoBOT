# Welcome to FumoBOT Tutorial

Welcome to FumoBOT Tutorial! This bot shows you how commands and functions work.
FumoBOT is organized into 6 main command categories:

## Tutorial Commands
- `.starter` - Kickstart your journey! Claim your starter pack of coins and gems.
- `.daily` or `.d` - Claim your daily bonus of coins and gems.
- `.library` or `.li` - Show the fumo you have discovered.
- `.inform` or `.in (NameOfFumo+rarity)` - Learn more about a fumo.
- `.sell` or `.s` - Sell fumos from your inventory.
- `.code` - Enter your code here!
- `.quest` or `.qu` - Show the quest.
- `.claim` or `.cl` - Claim the quest.

## Information Commands
- `.storage` or `.st` - View your fumo collection.
- `.balance` or `.b (@user/id)` - Check your coin/gems balance.
- `.items` or `.i` - View your item inventory.
- `.itemInfo` or `.it` - Show item details.
- `.use` or `.u` - Use an item.
- `.boost` or `.bst` - Show current boosts.
- `.craft` or `.c` - Show crafting recipes.

## Gamble Commands
- `.crategacha` or `.cg` - Crate gacha rolls.
- `.eventgacha` or `.eg (status)` - Event gacha rolls.
- `.pray` or `.p` - Daily prayer.
- `.slot` or `.sl` - Slot machine.
- `.gamble` or `.g` - Gamble coins.
- `.flip` or `.f (leaderboard)` - 50/50 coin flip.
- `.mysteryCrate` or `.mc` - Mystery crate.

## Shop Commands
- `.shop` or `.sh` - Main shop.
- `.market` or `.m` - Market.
- `.exchange` or `.e coins/gems` - Currency exchange.
- `.eggshop` or `.es` - Egg shop.

## Capitalism Commands
- `.addfarm` or `.af` - Add fumo to farm.
- `.farmcheck` or `.fc` - Check farm status.
- `.endfarm` or `.ef` - End farming.
- `.addbest` or `.ab` - Add all best fumos.
- `.farminfo` or `.fi` - View farm info.
- `.usefragment` or `.uf` - Increase farm slots.
- `.egginventory` or `.ei` - View eggs and pets.
- `.eggcheck` or `.ec` - Check egg hatching progress.
- `.useegg` or `.ue` - Cook an egg.
- `.equippet` or `.ep` - Equip a pet.

## Miscellaneous Commands
- `.leaderboard` or `.le` - Show top 3 players.
- `.report` - Report issues.
- `.credit` or `.cr` - Bot credits.
- `.otherCMD` - Show other commands.

and the sub-command having other categories such as:
- `.anime [nameAnime]` - Displays detailed information about an anime.
- `.play` - Play music of your choice.
- `.invite` - Invite the bot to another server.
- `.reddit` - Fetch a post from Reddit.
- `.groupInform` - Displays detailed information about the server.
- `.avatar help` - Shows information about a user; defaults to the command user if none mentioned.
- `.ping` - Checks the bot's latency and API latency.
- `.roleinfo [@role]` - Displays information about a specific role.
- `.leaderboard` - Displays top players or rankings.
- `.afk` - Set yourself as AFK.
- `.deathbattle [@username] [set-hp] [jjk / anime]` - Start a deathbattle with a friend using anime settings.
